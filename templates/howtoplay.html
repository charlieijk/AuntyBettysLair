HOME SIGNOUT

Movement:

  • Press W to move forward
  • Press S to move backward
  • Press A to move left
  • Press D to move right

Actions:

  • Press ↑ to shoot in the up direction
  • Press ↓ to shoot in the down direction
  • Press ← to shoot in the left direction
  • Press → to shoot in the right direction

Objectives:

    How to Win

  1. Navigate through the maze

    • Explore the maze and find the pathway to make it to the finish line
    • There will be a countdown judging on how far you try to make it to the maze...... so make sure you beat the countdown and get the highest score!

  2. Kill the pest

    • Locate and shoot the pest when it appears and respawns
    • The pest will spawn at various positions within the game

  3. Kill the demon

    • Locate and shoot the demon when it appears and respawns
    • The demon will spawn at various positions within the game

  • How To Lose

    • You lose all your health points!
    • You lose the countdown

    • Only Tip!

    • Dont focus on the time, get a high score !

    Happy Gaming!
